Instructions

This app allows you to simulate fantasy football games for the 2019-2020 NFL regular season

Create Team

  • Pick a total of 5 offensive players, one from each of the following positions:
    • Quarterback
    • Running Back
    • Tight End
    • Wide Receiver
    • Kicker

Roster

  • Click Release to release a player
  • Go back to Create Team to add more players
  • Players and be added or removed at anytime during the season

Season

  • Click Play Next Weeks Game to simulate the next game of the season
  • See stats for each game including:
    • Click on individual player's names to expand their individual stats and points
    • See team's weekly points in yellow
    • See total points for the season in the upper right hand corner
  • Click View Roster to go back to the roster to remove players
    • You can then go back Add Players and add another player of the same position as the removed player
  • Click Start Season Over to begin a new season

Scoring

  • Passing Yards: 1 point per 25 yards
  • Passing Touchdowns: 4 points
  • Passing Interceptions: -2 points
  • Rushing Yards: 1 point per 10 yards
  • Rushing Touchdowns: 6 points
  • Receptions: 1 points
  • Receiving Yards: 1 point per 10 yards
  • Receiving Touchdowns: 6 points
  • 2-Point Conversions: 2 points
  • Fumbles Lost: -2 points
  • Fumble Recovered for a Touchdown: 6 points
  • PAT Made: 1 point
  • FG Made (0-49 yards): 3 points
  • FG Made (50+ yards): 5 points
